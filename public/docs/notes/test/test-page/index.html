    Year numbering system #

    I use the Holocene Era or Human Era numbering system. It adds exactly 10,000 years to the currently dominant AD/BC or CE/BCE numbering system. It is denoted HE/BHE and allows for all key dates in human history to be listed using a simle increasing date scale - while keeping the transition from the CE calendar easy: you just add the digit “1” before the current year. Welcome to the year 12,020 HE!

    Examples:

    Human Era year Common Era year Event
    1001 HE 9000 BCE Jericho
    7301 HE 2700 BCE First pyramid
    11460 HE 1460 CE Machu Picchu built
    11945 HE 1945 CE United Nations founded
